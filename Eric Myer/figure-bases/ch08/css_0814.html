I'm an H2 and I'm proud!

The paragraphs that follow on will be separated as people expect, but not in the traditional manner: only the bottom margin is used to enforce this separation.

The top margins have been removed. This allows the first paragraph after an h2 (which has had its bottom margin removed) to get up close to the h2 without the use of an adjacent-sibling selector, which some browsers do not support.

Interparagraph spacing. Catch the wave!
